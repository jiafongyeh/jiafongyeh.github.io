Biography

Jia-Fong Yeh is a final-year PhD student in Computer Science at National Taiwan University (NTU), supervised by Prof. Winston Hsu. In addition, he also works closely with Prof. Yi-Ting Chen from National Yang Ming Chiao Tung University (NYCU).

His research interests include computer vision, deep robot learning, and evolutionary algorithms. He leads the policy learning team and co-leads the robotics team in Prof. Hsu's lab. Recently, he completed a ML internship at Sony Japan. Before joining NTU, Jia-Fong earned B.S. and M.S. degrees from National Taiwan Normal University (NTNU). From 2016 to 2022, he worked with Prof. Tsung-Che Chiang and Prof. Shun-Shii Lin.

He is a graduate member of IEEE and received the NOVATEK Ph.D. Scholarship (2022), NeurIPS Scholar Award (2024), NTUEE-1975 Technology Research Innovation Award (2025), and Hon Hai Technology Award (2025).

* Open to research discussions or collaborations.
